Your Weekend Watching Guide

Looking for something to watch this weekend? Here are six films we love!

A Hip-Hop Cult Classic

DMX and Nas star as a pair of hustlers dangerously striving for salvation in music-video visionary Hype Williams’s lone feature to date. ENDS NOV 30!

Con Games

This deliciously dark pulp thriller stars John Cusack, Annette Bening, and Anjelica Huston as con artists enmeshed in their own double crosses.

Noir at Its Most Sophisticated

A detective investigating the murder of an ad executive finds himself falling in love with the dead woman—until a shocking revelation upends the case.

Gothic Grandeur

Francis Ford Coppola enlists an all-star cast (Gary Oldman, Winona Ryder, Anthony Hopkins, Keanu Reeves) for a dazzling take on the horror classic.

Hollywood Uncensored

One of the most notorious films of the pre-Code era, this steamy melodrama stars Dorothy Mackaill as a sex worker on the run.

Immigrant Women Between Worlds

Lina Rodriguez’s acclaimed documentary follows three Latin American immigrants to Canada, reflecting their fragmented and hybrid identities.
