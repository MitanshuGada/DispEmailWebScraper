$999—Weeklong guided tour of Peru w/flights, save $2100
By Casey Gasinowski  |  Source: Exoticca

What's the big deal? Explore Peru's must-see destinations—Machu Picchu, the Sacred Valley, Lima and Cusco—on this 7-night guided tour. With this Black Friday offer from Exoticca, you'll save up to $2100 vs. similar trips we've researched.

For $999 per person—the lowest price we've seen for this trip this year—here is what's included:

  • Roundtrip international flights from 7 U.S. cities to Lima on select dates in January-June, September-October & December 2024
  • Domestic flights between Lima and Cusco
  • 7 nights in well-reviewed hotels with daily breakfast (3 nights in Lima, 2 nights in the Sacred Valley, 2 nights in Cusco)
  • Expedition train from the Sacred Valley to Aguas Calientes
  • Transfers in air-conditioned vehicles
  • Tours and entrance fees, including Machu Picchu tour, Lima & Cusco city tours and Sulca Textile Museum visit
  • English-speaking tour guide
  • All taxes and fees
  • For +$149, add a protection plan that allows for free cancellation of your trip for any reason up to 30 days before departure; you'll get 100% of the trip cost back in travel credit

You can customize your trip with hotel upgrades, plus add-on activities and meals. You can also arrange to do this trip as a private tour. Additional departure cities are available, starting at +$100.

Other Peru vacations are also part of this sale, including an 9-night tour that includes a visit to Lake Titicaca, the largest lake in South America; an 11-night trip featuring Arequipa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and Colca Canyon, the second-deepest canyon in the world; and an 11-night adventure that includes a stay in the Amazon.

To book by phone, call 888-273-1533 from Monday-Friday: 9 a.m.-midnight ET; Saturday-Sunday: 8 a.m.-9 p.m. Mention the following vacation codes when calling: 7 nights (7452), 9 nights (6948), 11 nights including Lake Titicaca (16528) and 11 nights including the Amazon (7707).
