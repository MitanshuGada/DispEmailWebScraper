  • The use of harsh and authoritarian verbal discipline has been shown to have adverse effects on a child's emotional and psychological well-being.


  • Research reported in The Community Mental Health Journal (2019) identified that parenting styles have a direct impact on aggression in children.


  • Authoritarian or "because I told you so" parenting styles are categorized as demanding, extremely strict and uncompromising, ignoring the child's point of view.


  • Children raised in an environment dominated by authoritarian parenting, especially marked by harsh verbal discipline, are more likely to exhibit behavioral problems during adolescence.


  • Beyond behavioral problems, children subjected to harsh verbal discipline within an authoritarian environment parenting framework, face a higher risk of developing depressive disorders.


  • As parents, caregivers and society at large, it is crucial to recognize the importance of fostering a nurturing communicative environment that promotes positive reinforcement and healthy emotional development in children.
