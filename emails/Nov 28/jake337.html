New research paves way for Artificial Intelligence in education (UK Gov) The UK's Department for Education's first-ever Call for Evidence on AI in Education, involving over 500 respondents, highlights the growing embrace of AI in the sector for tasks like administrative efficiency, personalized learning support, and interactive lessons, while also addressing potential risks and guiding future policy.
