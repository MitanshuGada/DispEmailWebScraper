Now Two Paths to ISSMP
Now there are two ways to earn the ISSMP. This new option makes the ISSMP accessible to more professionals seeking certification that specifically demonstrates their advanced knowledge, skills and abilities in the fields of security management.
  • Path 1: CISSPs in good standing must have a minimum of two years of cumulative full-time experience in one or more of the five domains in the ISSMP Exam Outline
  • Path 2: All other candidates must have a minimum of seven years of cumulative full-time experience in two or more of the five domains in the ISSMP Exam Outline. See full details.
