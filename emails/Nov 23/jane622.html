To our PROVEN community,

 

As we gather around the Thanksgiving table this year, we want to express our deep gratitude for each and every one of you. 

 

At PROVEN, we’re not just a skincare brand—we’re a community of dreamers who believe in the power of science, innovation, and the transformative impact of personalized solutions.

 

Our journey began with a simple idea: that personalized skincare could change lives for the better. Time and again, we experienced the frustration of waiting months to see specialists, spending exorbitant amounts on treatments, only to find that tailored formulas were the key to unlocking our skin’s potential. 

 

This quest led us to build an AI-powered database, harness the collective knowledge of thousands of scientific studies, and partner with the brilliant minds at Stanford’s dermatology department. We embarked on this journey not only to improve our own lives but to make a meaningful impact on the lives of others.

 

For all of this and more, we want to say thank you—for believing in us, for entrusting us with your unique skin journeys, and for standing with us in the pursuit of better, healthier, and more radiant skin. 

 

We wish you a holiday season filled with moments of warmth, joy, and love—whether you spend them with family, friends, or simply with yourself.

 

Happy Thanksgiving,

Ming and Amy

PROVEN cofounders
