Green Friday with Gumtree: savvy and sustainable shopping just a street or two away!
The UK alone generates 26 million tonnes of waste each year* and consumption is one of our biggest challenges when it comes to the climate crisis.
At Gumtree, we're all about reducing waste, sharing more and encouraging more sustainable shopping habits. Whatsmore, when you shop second hand you can bag a bargain to match those Black Friday deals!

Sustainable Shopping 
Black Friday deals can be tempting..but did you know you can find new, nearly new and as good as new on Gumtree? Why not make Gumtree your go-to platform for finding pre-loved treasures this shopping season, from furniture to electronics, clothing to toys.
