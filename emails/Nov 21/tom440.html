Smoldering Drama on a Seaside Vacation

In Christian Petzold’s acclaimed latest, a self-absorbed novelist (Thomas Schubert) on vacation by the Baltic Sea finds himself unexpectedly sharing a vacation home with a mysterious young woman (Paula Beer). As the pair grow closer, an encroaching forest fire threatens them and pushes the writer to discover whether he can truly care for anything beyond himself.
 


Plus: a new interview with Petzold, the latest installment in our Meet the Filmmakers series!
