Stay a third night for free at 17 Santa Barbara hotels
By Camille Guzman

Why you should go: The picturesque coastal city of Santa Barbara is a hub of cheer this season. You’ll find pop-up and seasonal markets, festive parades and holiday-themed performances at historic theaters downtown. That’s all on top of its year-round world-class cuisine, Mediterranean-style architecture and uncrowded beaches. Getting there is easy via nonstop flights from Oakland, San Francisco and Sacramento on Southwest or United. Alternatively, drive south along the coast.

What’s the big deal? This sale gets you a third night free when you pay for two at 17 area hotels—including a few we haven’t seen discounted in a while. Stay options include winter, spring and even summer dates, depending on the property. Choose from modern downtown hotels, 5-star beachfront retreats, family- and budget-friendly accommodations and quaint B&Bs.

Note: Booking windows, promo codes and restrictions vary by hotel, so be sure to read the fine print.
