Introducing Chaké - The Magic System of Meet Dyoro

Chaké is the lifeblood of our story, connecting tribes with extraordinary elemental powers that shape the fabric of their existence. With four elemental archetypes—Tesi, Firi, Denki, and Yeye—Chaké intricately weaves together a unique blend of MBTI personality types and cognitive functions to create a rich, multilayered magic system.

 

Each tribe holds its own chaké, safely guarded within a sacred stone or natural element. The four elemental archetypes come in Introverted and Extraverted versions, allowing for a fascinating array of sensing, intuitive, thinking, and feeling powers. In rare instances, tribes may even possess a Duality version, combining both Introverted and Extraverted elements of an archetype.
