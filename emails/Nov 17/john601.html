I was sitting there with my wife, talking about how the kids are going respectively


Isn’t it crazy how different each of them are, i bet you feel the same with yours


And we were reflecting and comparing memories of when our eldest was the same age as the younger two


And then it just hit both of us at the same time


It’s a weird thing, moving through life


Little changes stack up with grey hairs and a few more wrinkles


But man


The speed of irreversible change is real when we look at our kids


And of course thats supposed to happen, they’re growing


But it was almost like a sorrow that we felt


Because what’s happening is exactly that - irreversible


You can’t even slow down time, let alone stop it


Have you tried to sit there and watch the clock


I have lately, it seemed so slow and painful when i was young


But now, when i watched the seconds hand the other day


It was insane


Do it, give it a try, it’s pretty scary man


That seconds hand was moving SO FAST


And every second i watched, disappeared, gone


Into the abyss of the eternal “past”


I felt an anxiety come over me talking with my wife


It hit me, and i’m the perspective king


The fact that every age and stage of our children will come to pass


And we'll be old 


Things will be remembered


Things will be forgotten


We will die


And the world keeps turning


I’m not here to plead or beg you


But fuck mate, DO NOT relinquish the opportunity you have


To go ALL IN on your life and your family


I know death is coming for me, as it is for you


But what i also know is that with a heartbeat and head


I can do something about my “now”


And you can too


Don’t waste it man


The greatest and most rewarding thing we can do in this world is grow and develop


Become better than we were last month, last year


And channel that into the leadership and experiences we provide for our children 


So that they remain connected to us on a deeper level over the years


But also so that they want to as well, because you are them and they are you


Life is precious, it’s strange to think of what happens after


No name, no identity, no more reality inside your life with you and your kids


Why would any man want to waste it right now then?


Give your kids a hug, tell them you love them


Go out and hunt, provide for your family


But don’t lose yourself and your family in the process


And the only way that can ever happen is evolution


To become more than you are so you can have more than you’ve got


What a crazy game it is


Til tomorrow


Claim today


Al “grateful & hungry” Ranieri
