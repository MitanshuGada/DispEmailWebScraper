Stop By Winterland for Even More Rides, Games and Holiday Fun for All Ages

It’s finally here! We're so excited to officially kick off Wintrust Winterland at Gallagher Way! Join us for all kinds of family-friendly activities and new interactive experiences that are returning to both Gallagher Way and inside historic Wrigley Field. 

Don’t forget! Wintrust Winterland at Gallagher Way’s hours for Thanksgiving week are as follows:
Tuesday, November 21: 11 a.m. to 9 p.m.
Wednesday, November 22: 11 a.m. to 9 p.m.
Thursday, November 23: 11 a.m. to 4 p.m.

​​​​

Wintrust Tree Lighting Ceremony

Celebrate the season tonight at 5 p.m. with the Wintrust Tree Lighting Ceremony and the official opening of Wintrust Winterland at Gallagher Way. Join us for an evening of holiday cheer with a special musical performance from the Chicago Gay Men's Chorus and appearances from a few Cubs legends, followed by the lighting of the tree.

Between 4 and 9 p.m., donate your gently used hats, coats, gloves and scarves to Cradles to Crayons. Their cruiser will be parked at the south end of Gallagher Way, near the Wrigley Field ticket office, collecting donations. ​​​​​

Christkindlmarket Wrigleyville

Step into a whimsical wonderland as soon as you arrive at Christkindlmarket Wrigleyville. Experience an array of market vendors and food and beverage offerings, as well as the new 40-foot Ferris wheel with a view of Gallagher Way’s dazzling tree. Enjoy the traditional favorites you’ve been waiting for, including the candy cane-striped wooden huts, festive shopping options, international delicacies, hot spiced Glühwein, fine beers, hand-crafted ornaments, and more.

The Lodge

The Lodge is always a cozy place to land and grab a drink or a snack while enjoying the festivities at Wintrust Winterland at Gallagher Way. This year, the après-ski-style Lodge has gotten some new additions and is now serving your favorite ballpark fare in addition to festive beverages and s’mores kits to roast by the outdoor firepits.

The Lodge also has a full calendar of events! On select evenings, join us for bingo, themed trivia and live music. 

Holiday Music Bingo
December 5 and December 19 from 6:30 to 8 p.m.

Themed Trivia
November 28 (Elf trivia), December 12 (Taylor Swift trivia), and December 26 (2023 trivia) from 6:30 to 8 p.m.

Live Music
November 30, December 7, December 21 and December 28 from 6 to 8 p.m.
