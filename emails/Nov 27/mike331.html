In today’s newsletter, we discover a newly found toll of highly addictive ultra-processed foods; explore efforts to stop the predator eating coral reefs, discover a cursed gem at the heart of a best-seller ... and ask: why has society picked on short men?

This 1,600-calorie burger won’t kill me, right? Or frozen pizza? Or a 48-ingredient bar sold as nutritious?

They are all ultra-processed foods, and new studies show a diet of such addictive, highly advertised foods can bring on anxiety, depression, and cognitive decline.

Yes, they could take your mind.

How can we protect ourselves?
