Automated captions are great when it comes to speed and privacy, but they can sometimes miss on accuracy, especially with non-common words.

One of the best things about Nagish is that you can improve the quality of your captions by maintaining a personal dictionary - you can add names, unique words, or phrases that might be a bit uncommon. 

Just tap through to Settings -> Call Preferences -> Language & Tone, and you can make our captions even better by personalizing them for you!
