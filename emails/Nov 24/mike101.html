4 integrations to use to create the best customer experiences


Want to unlock the power of integrations? Here are four ways to combine your favorite tools with Hotjar to boost your workflow, get more insights, and grow your business.

1. Understand how users navigate your online store with Shopify


So you’ve built a beautiful ecommerce store with Shopify. Woohoo! What next?


To increase conversions and create an enjoyable user experience (UX), you need to see what actually happens when people land on your site. Sure, you want customers to add several products to their basket and then effortlessly glide to the checkout—but it might not be so easy for them.


Combine Hotjar with Shopify to

  • 🗺️ Reveal where users click, scroll, and move via visual representations of aggregated data with Heatmaps. Understand which parts of your site, content, and product pages hold people’s attention, and which elements (like CTA buttons) get overlooked.

  • 📹 Watch playbacks of real user behavior with Recordings to learn how people engage with key pages. Filter by rage clicks to discover areas of frustration, or jump from bug reports to the associated session recording to see exactly what went wrong.

  • 📋 Gather valuable feedback with Surveys to gauge customer satisfaction (CSAT) and get ideas for improvements. Run on-site surveys to ask about their experience, like exit-intent surveys to understand why they abandoned a purchase, or send a post-purchase survey via email to collect testimonials.

2. Get a deeper understanding of your prospects and customers with HubSpot


Want to empower your customer-facing teams with deeper customer knowledge and fuel more personalized journeys?


Sales, marketing, and support teams use HubSpot to keep track of all of their information about leads and prospects. By sending behavior insights from Hotjar—like session recordings and survey responses—right to the associated contact timeline in HubSpot, you can create a seamless one-stop shop with all of the info you need to tailor your outreach efforts to your customer’s unique needs.


Use Hotjar and HubSpot together to

  • Empower your sales team with recordings of their prospect’s user journey for more context, so they can personalize their conversations and close more leads

  • Trigger marketing flows based on user behavior recorded by both HubSpot and Hotjar to deliver conversion-focused messaging in the right channel, at the right time

  • Monitor customer satisfaction by adding CSAT or Net Promoter Score (NPS) survey responses to your contact timeline

3. Share key customer insights cross-functionally with Slack


1 unread notification: here’s how to delight your customers. If only it were that simple, right?


With the Hotjar-Slack integration, it can be. 😎 Connect Hotjar with Slack to get real-time updates whenever you receive a new session recording, feedback response, or survey reply—right in your team workspace.


Here’s how to turn your Slack channels into a powerhouse of customer insights:

  • Determine which data you want to focus on. Use segments and filters to define which data gets sent to your Slack channel based on what matters to your team. For example, surface recordings with rage clicks or u-turns to spot common pain points or bugs, or create a dedicated channel to track feedback from customers in a new market.

  • Tag stakeholders and discuss your next steps in Threads. @mention relevant people and make decisions quickly to resolve issues faster, all from one central place.

  • Bring real user insights into everyone’s daily workflow. Deliver valuable data straight to shared, centralized Slack channels for increased visibility, transparency, and empathy, and enable every team—from product to customer support—to stay close to the voice of the customer

4. Catch, log, and squash bugs faster than ever with Jira


Nothing degrades customer trust—or, eek, deters a sale 😱—like a pesky bug. Make it easier for your devs team to fix issues before they become problems by sending data straight from Hotjar to their favorite bug-tracking tool, like Jira.

Use the Hotjar-Jira integration to

  • Streamline the bug-reporting process. Create a Jira ticket from a session recording, console error, or feedback response in Hotjar, automatically including everything your developers need to contextualize the bug and prioritize a fix.

  • Empower everyone on your team to log issues. Catch more errors and help your developers focus on fixing bugs, not finding them.

  • Deliver a better UX for your customers. More efficient workflows behind the scenes mean faster resolutions—and happier customers.

In a nutshell 🥜


Bring out the best in your tech stack by integrating the tools you love and use every day. Connect your favorite software to:

  • 🤩 Observe and improve the user journey on your ecommerce store

  • 🤓 Get valuable context that enriches your prospect outreach and sales conversations

  • 🔍 Quickly spot, share, and take action on important issues

  • 🐛 Identify and fix bugs to deliver a better user experience

By streamlining your workflow, you save hours of clicking between disconnected apps looking for the right data, improving efficiency while still staying laser-focused on the customer experience.


The result? More time to focus on what really matters: customer-centric improvements that drive real business growth.
