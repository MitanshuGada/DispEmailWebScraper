Value Research has an exclusive series– Money Masters– to give a glimpse into how industry veterans approach investing. In the series, Dhirendra Kumar interviews the top fund managers from AMCs across the country.

At Aditya Birla, he has managed every kind of fund. While he predominantly oversees large-cap funds, he has exposure to the mid- and small-cap universe. Even now, he gets excited about finding an undiscovered gem without significant growth potential.

He shares an instance where he found a quality business with growth potential trading at reasonable valuations. In the case of Titan, he found the company early on its journey when it was trading at a P/E multiple of just 30. Now, the stock trades at a P/E of 90!

Similarly, he revealed to us the three key factors he looks for in a great business. Also, if you are curious about which kind you should avoid, he answers that too.
