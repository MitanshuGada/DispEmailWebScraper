Platinum 3776 Uroko Gumo

Platinum introduces the Fuji Unkei, Uroko-Gumo, the inaugural piece in their new #3776 fountain pen series, limited to 3,000 pens worldwide. The translucent barrel and deep blue-grey cap represent the atmospheric beauty of Mt. Fuji, making it the first bicolour #3776 in the collection. The rhodium-plated 14-carat gold nib is meticulously crafted in-house and undergoes thorough hand-testing at the factory. Each pen is presented in a carefully designed gift box, complete with a black ink cartridge, a converter, and a bottle of Aka-Fuji ink, characterized by its captivating red hue with a greenish sheen.
Platinum 3776 Uroko Gumo

Platinum 3776 Sands of Komodo

The Sands of Komodo is the latest addition to the North American Platinum lineup, belonging to the #3776 Century model. Inspired by the Komodo Islands' pristine pink beaches and tropical blue waters, the pen features a translucent light blue body with rose gold trim and a 14k rose gold nib. The design aims to transport users to their personal paradise. Leveraging Platinum's advanced technology, the #3776 Century model introduces the "Slip & Seal" mechanism in its inventive cap design, ensuring ink vibrancy and preventing drying out for an impressive period of up to 24 months.
Platinum 3776 Sands of Komodo

Platinum Izumo Available in 2 Versions
 
Platinum introduces the new Izumo nib, featuring an 18k gold design with the Izumo logo elegantly stamped below the breather hole and Izumo clouds gracing the nib's tines. The Platinum Iro Urushi fountain pens highlight the captivating Izumo design, known for its luxurious curves and seamless transitions from convex to concave in the cap profile and barrel. Inspired by artwork from the Endo period, the Iro Urushi, meaning colored urushi, serves as a wellspring of inspiration. The fountain pen is available in Budou Nezu, which is a grey/lavender color, or the dark teal Aitetsu.
Platinum Izumo
