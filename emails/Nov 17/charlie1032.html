Serenity
Serenity is where we put the "ahhh" in "adult" — the onboard spot where kids aren't allowed, leaving grown-ups to kick back, soak up some sun and... isn't that sounding nice already? There's a bar nearby, so grab a drink, a good book or your partner and get ready to do some serious adulting.

Bars
A Carnival cruise gives you all kinds of reasons to raise a glass, so it's good to know there's variety in the bars too! Indoors or out, from sports bars to wine bars, rum spots to tequila joints, brewpubs to mixology labs... you'll find bars stocked with your vibe and your drink of choice.
