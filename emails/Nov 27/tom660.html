The December SCTE event is here!
SCTE® LiveLearning for Professionals Webinar™ Series: Going to 10G & Beyond
Date: Thursday, December 14th
Time: 11:00 AM ET
 
As another busy year of technology development winds down, the cable industry is starting to deliver on its promise to offer multi-gig speeds to subscribers over its legacy HFC network. Leading MSOs like Comcast and Altice USA are rolling out 2 Gig and faster service, lab and field trials of DOCSIS 4.0 are moving forward, and vendors are developing the equipment and software to support higher speeds and greater bandwidth demands. In this year-end session, we will look at where cable stands nearly five years into the 10G era!

Questions to be discussed:
  • What comes next in the 10G evolution?
  • What will the new year bring for cable's 10G quest?
  • What challenges must still be overcome?
  • And more!
