There’s a spot for you, John!
 
Join us for our Identity + Inclusion Affinity Club Panel on December 6. There’s still time to register for this event!

This panel will provide you with more information about MIT Sloan student clubs and give you insight into specific student experiences. Hear from members of the following clubs:
  • MIT Sloan Pride Club
  • MIT Sloan Women in Management (SWIM)
  • Black Business Students Association (BBSA)
  • African Business Club (ABC)
  • Asian American Alliance (AAA)
  • Hispanic Business Club (HBC)
  • First Generation/Low Income Club (FLI @ Sloan)
This panel will include topics such as MIT Sloan’s community, resources for career success, and alumni network. If you missed any of the previous sessions in this series, check out our Identity + Inclusion YouTube playlist to access our recorded events.

Please note: While the event content is focused on the perspectives and experiences of specific communities, all are welcome to attend.
