Your Support Matters!

Thank you to everyone who has donated, shared our posts, forwarded emails, and left kind messages on this #GivingTuesday. You are creating a lasting impact for the warriors, veterans, and families of Naval Special Warfare (NSW).  

If you haven't given yet, here are examples of what your donation can do:

  • $5,000 covers immediate tragedy assistance funds for the family of a fallen NSW service member.
  • $2,500 funds an education scholarship to a member of the NSW community.
  • $1,000 sends one NSW child to a specialized summer camp.
  • $500 gives a computer to the child of a fallen SEAL.
  • $100 helps cover the cost of NSW events like graduation ceremonies and spouse all-calls.
  • $50 helps fund a SEAL's attendance at a transition networking event.
  • $20 pays for one therapeutic yoga session for a member of NSW.
