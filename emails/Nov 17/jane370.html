Finance Your Dream Disney Vacation for As Low As 0% Interest for 12 Months with DVC Rental Store
 

DVC Rental Store has taken affordability to the next level by partnering with Bread Pay™, a financing solution that allows you to pay for your Disney Vacation over time with competitive interest rates.

Experience the Magic with Bread Pay™ Financing
 

The DVC Rental Store understands that vacations are an investment in memories and happiness, which is why we’ve teamed up with Bread Pay™ to make your Disney Vacation more accessible. Through Bread Pay™, you can enjoy the following benefits:

 
  1. Affordable Monthly Plans: With Bread Pay™, you can break down the cost of your Disney Vacation into manageable monthly payments. By spreading the expense over time, you can better plan your budget and still enjoy the magical experience of Disney.
  2. Competitive Interest Rates: Bread Pay™ offers competitive interest rates, ensuring that your monthly payments are not only affordable but also cost-effective.
  3. No Prepayment Penalties: Should you find yourself in a position to pay off your financing earlier than expected, Bread Pay™ allows you to do so without any penalties. This flexibility can save you even more on interest costs.
  4. Easy Application: Bread Pay™ simplifies the financing process with an easy online application that provides a decision in seconds, without affecting your credit score. Your data is also encrypted and secure throughout the process.
  5. Check Your Rate Without Leaving the DVC Rental Store Website: You can get a quick estimate of your financing options without leaving the DVC Rental Store site, making it easier than ever to plan your dream Disney Vacation.

How to Get Started

To take advantage of Bread Pay™ financing for your Disney Vacation Club rental, visit the DVC Rental Store website and follow these simple steps:
  1. Browse available DVC rental listings and choose your dream Disney Vacation.
  2. When you're ready to book, look for the Bread Pay™ financing option during the checkout process.
  3. Click on the Bread Pay™ option to apply for financing. The application takes only a few moments and won't impact your credit score.
  4. If approved, select your preferred payment plan and finalize your booking.
