So here we go…

Just 3 and half years ago, I had never even touched the stock market.

Actually, back then I had no job, little money and about 100,000 dollars in debt.

I had worked at a non-profit for over 20 years, where I met my wife, and we lived in a cramped room, barely making enough money for even that.

But as I approached the age of 42, it hit me like a ton of bricks: “I want to start a family and have kids!”

The problem was that there was simply no way we could afford to raise a child on top of what we were already struggling to pay for.

The even bigger problem was that time was running out. I was 42, and my wife was 34. If we wanted a family, we had to make a change REALLY fast.

I had a random range of skills up my sleeve, such as woodworking and carpentry, but nothing that would make a change fast enough for us to have a baby before we got too old. To be honest, it felt hopeless.

But then something happened.

I crashed at my brother's place while my wife wrapped things up in another city. Coincidentally, during that time, my brother was trading stocks and options (something I had no interest in), but because we were hanging out, I decided to pay attention.

One morning, he bought a Tesla option for about $300. The next thing blew my mind.

By the end of the day, the value of this option had gone up to a point where he had made an extra $300. 100% profit!!! WHAT?

“How is that possible? I don’t understand.” But I was hooked.

So, over the next few weeks, I ended up spending a lot of time watching charts just for fun, wondering if I could learn and also make some profit.

That’s when I noticed something really strange… that I didn’t know was going to end up changing my life.

Everyday, around 9:34 am Eastern time, a particular stock would perform a small jump. Every single day. Like clockwork.

I shared my discovery with an experienced stock trader, asking him if some stocks indeed had predictable patterns.

He laughed at me and said “Not possible. If anyone could predict the market even a little, they would be incredibly successful.”

…I should have given up right then and there but I couldn’t help myself: I could SEE this pattern!

What if he was wrong… and I was right?

So, I began closely analyzing this daily jump, searching for any potential signals or indicators that might precede it.

After a few more weeks, I finally cracked the code!
Here’s the secret, the missing piece of the puzzle…

Another big index would always move up right before that predictable jump. That was it!

So, I thought to myself, "Why not give it a shot? All I have to do is buy the stock right before the jump and sell it right after.”

But there was another problem… By this point, I had almost completely run out of money.

I had a card with about $1,900 of credit left on it.

So, I made a bold choice. I took a $1,200 cash advance with a 27.9% interest rate attached to it and deposited the money directly into a trading account.

My aim was to make around $60 each day, just enough to cover essential expenses like food and gas.

I was either digging my own grave or finally getting out of it.

My first trade was on February 18th, 2020.

I waited for the pattern to present itself and I nervously bought a call option for 1,080 dollars just beforehand.

The following minutes felt like a lifetime…but the jump happened exactly as I predicted and…I sold for exactly 1,130 dollars.

I made my first $50 using the predictable pattern!

I was beyond happy and to this day, that first trade on this pattern was the best I have ever felt trading!

So, the next day, I did the same thing. And I made $199!

Then on my next trade I made 300 bucks!

The next day, I made $185.

Over the next few months, I continued trading the same predictable pattern (plus, I discovered a few other similar ones!), and was withdrawing money to cover expenses.

Fast forward to today, I’m trading with over hundreds of thousands in my account and making as much as $3000 in around 20 minutes!

I cleared my debts, we bought our own house in LA and…

The best day of my life happened. I don’t cry often but…I did on this day. We had our son, Owen!

All of this because of that one “secret pattern”!
