This Week's Inside Scoop: 11.28.23
It is amazing how quickly an hour goes by in the presence of elephants. I have been to Africa more than 20 times, and I still remember watching one elephant family with a brand-new baby. At first, he stayed tucked under his mother, but then he ventured out a little. He had no idea what to do with his trunk and kept playing with it, kicking it with his foot and winding it around his face—just getting to know it. To be able to be there and watch that was such a gift. It made my heart glow.

I was reminded of this baby—and lion cubs, too!—when I watched our most popular slideshow on Tanzania, created by Glenn Gillaspie, 6-time traveler from Beverly Hills, CA. Glen divides his slideshow into little chapters, which of course include wonderful photos of animals—but I appreciate that he begins with the people. From members of the Maasai tribe to our incredible Trip Experience Leaders and driver-guides, the people of Tanzania have truly captured my heart.

When you join us for Safari Serengeti: Tanzania Lodge & Tented Safari, you will spend a day in a Maasai village, meeting members of the tribe and participating in their traditions. And of course Tanzania’s wildlife will be on full display, from the Serengeti plains, teeming with massive herds of wildebeest, to the idyllic floor of Ngorongoro Crater.

Also this week, we share our most popular NEW adventures for 2024, some of which visit brand-new destinations for O.A.T.—including the Gambia, Switzerland, Bulgaria, and more.

Has a wildlife encounter ever stopped you in your tracks? Email your stories to me at harriet@gct.com.
