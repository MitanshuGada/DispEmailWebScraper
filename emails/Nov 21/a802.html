Welcome to the inaugural issue of Athlete By Design

Dive deep into the world of elite sports as we uncover the dedication, grit, and raw talent behind some of the globe's finest athletes.

Our first spotlight shines on the remarkable Zoe Smith, an English weightlifter who etched her name in history by being the first English woman to clinch a Commonwealth Games weightlifting medal. From her groundbreaking win in 2010 in Delhi to her tenacious spirit at the 2020 Summer Olympics, Zoe embodies the essence of perseverance.

But what fuels such unwavering determination? In this edition, we unravel the products that power Zoe — whether she's breaking records on the platform or unwinding after a rigorous training session.

Step into the life of Zoe Smith and discover the tools that sculpt champions, only on Athlete By Design.

Stay driven and inspired, everyone! These products play a role in my journey, and perhaps they could find a place in yours too.

With determination,
Zoe Smith - Team GB Athlete
