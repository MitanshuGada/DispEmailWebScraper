In this audio meditation, I am guiding you through a calming visualization exercise, especially created for the often very busy and sometimes hectic Thanksgiving Travel Time. 

Leaning on the imagery of the poem "Over the River and Through the Woods," you will escape into a soft relaxation where you will easily be able to let go of any tension about your upcoming travels. 


Listen to the Meditation


Join me and imagine the airport and your upcoming plane ride as a bustling modern sleigh ride, with skilled pilots ensuring your safe journey.

As the plane ascends, anxiety is addressed and replaced with a sense of love and warmth. Finally, as the plane descends, you are reminded of the joy and celebration that awaits you at your destination.
