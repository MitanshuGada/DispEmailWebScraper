Wish Book and Share the Spirit 2023

In the coming weeks, we will publish this year’s Wish Book and Share the Spirit articles highlighting the work that two dozen South Bay and East Bay social service agencies do to help those in need in our communities. You’ll read about people overcoming addiction, how a garden gave someone a reason to get out of bed each day, how a refurbished bicycle can transform a life, about the accomplishments of athletes in wheelchairs, and many more stories of help and hope.

The Mercury News Wish Book is celebrating its 40th year of collecting donations that grant financial wishes for social service agencies that provide essential services across the South Bay. By contributing to East Bay Times Share the Spirit, you are offering assistance to agencies serving a wide range of residents across the East Bay.
