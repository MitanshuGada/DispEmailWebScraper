A more fair, affordable Canada is coming.

Dear Jake, 

A milestone moment is hanging in the balance.

After years of relentless campaigning, hard work, and your unwavering support, we are on the cusp of a monumental victory. The Canadian government's recent economic statement promises deep pro-competition, monopoly-busting reforms – new reforms including huge new powers for our Competition Bureau to study and block monopolies, and even a right for ordinary people like you to make a case if a monopoly harms you.1 What’s that mean?  It’s a huge chance to prevent the next buyout fiasco like Rogers-Shaw, and to make Canada's telecom sector AND every other part of our economy more affordable and fair. This is not just a tantalizingly close win; it's the fulfillment of our collective dream!

Help us finish the fight!

Remember the Rogers-Shaw saga? How more than 80,000 of you stood united against it? We may have lost that battle – but we’re on track to win the war. Our sustained efforts – YOUR voice! – have influenced a major policy shift. Today, we have a real chance to shape Canada's telecom landscape, and every other sector dominated by monopolies, for decades. But the work isn't over yet. In the next month, we'll witness a defining battle – a battle that will decide whether our telecom industry sees fair prices and innovative service, or wilts under steadily increasing monopolies.

We need to bring this one home. We're launching a hot new action very soon: demanding MPs across all parties to support competition reform Bill C-56 with the strongest possible amendments. This is our golden opportunity to ensure a fair, competitive, and affordable telecom market. But to succeed, we need to keep the momentum going. We need to sustain the fight, and make sure they’re hearing new pressure from THOUSANDS more Canadians. If our voice fails us– the lobbyists are going to drown ordinary people out.

Your contribution will directly support our efforts to mobilize Canadians, engage with policymakers, and keep the pressure on MPs. Every dollar you give brings us closer towards a telecom industry and Canadian economy that works for everyone, not just the big players. Will you stand with us in this crucial hour? Your support could be the difference between an unprecedented triumph and regression to our sluggish, monopoly driven status quo. 

Support the fight for a fair Canada!

For fairness,

Matt Hatfield, Executive Director, OpenMedia
