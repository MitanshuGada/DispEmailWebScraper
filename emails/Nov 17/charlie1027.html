U.S. Cannabis Employment Report 2023

We have seen a whirlwind of cannabis employment activity as new markets enter the scene.

 

Cannabis markets are active in 24 adult-use states and territories (25 when Ohio goes recreational in December) and 39 medical-only states.

 

Cannabis job growth has been impressive compared to job growth in established industries like finance and construction.

 

Cannabis jobs now outnumber jobs in other essential industries, with no signs of slowing down.

 

Cannabis Job Growth - 2017-2023

  • 2017 - 122,280 jobs
  • 2018 - 149,300 jobs
  • 2019 - 211,000 jobs
  • 2020 - 243,700 jobs
  • 2021 - 321,000 jobs
  • 2022 - 428,059 jobs
  • 2023 - 418,000 jobs

What were the most in-demand cannabis jobs in 2023?

  • Cannabis Cultivation
  • Cannabis Manufacturing
  • Cannabis Retail
  • Cannabis Distribution
  • Cannabis Testing
  • Cannabis Infrastructure Management
  • Cannabis Human Resources

Cannabis Job Salaries & Cannabis Employment Report 

 

Cannabis companies look for experienced and talented employees, leading to competitive job salaries and benefits packages.

 

However, cannabis job salaries vary on many factors, including company size, location, and your skills and experience.

 

In 2022, cannabis salaries increased by 4% and up to 10% for senior executives.
