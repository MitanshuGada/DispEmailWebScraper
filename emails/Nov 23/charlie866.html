Dear Neighbor,
 
This past month, we observed Breast Cancer Awareness Month, an important period for promoting cancer survivorship and finding a cure. As someone deeply committed to this cause, I firmly support it. I will continue to raise awareness, extend my support to survivors, and contribute to research initiatives. Together, we can bring hope, make progress, and create a brighter future for those impacted by cancer.
 
November is a month of gratitude, and I am grateful every day for God, my family, our health, and the privilege of serving you. On this Thanksgiving, I wish you and your loved ones a truly wonderful holiday. May you celebrate with family, friends, and cherished ones.
 
Thank you for your confidence and trust.
 
Warm regards,


Kionne McGhee
Commissioner, Miami-Dade County District 9

Observing Domestic Violence Month


Advocating for domestic violence survivors and shining a light on this pervasive issue is of the utmost importance to me. By doing so, we save countless individuals who may be trapped in the darkness of abuse. It is our duty to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to live a safe and fulfilling life, free from fear and full of success. I want to express my deepest gratitude to Kat Allen for her tireless advocacy and unwavering commitment to this cause. Through her efforts, she is helping to mend the broken pieces and restore hope in the lives of survivors. Together, we can bring about lasting change and create a society where no one suffers in silence. Let us stand united, raise our voices, and work towards a future where every individual can thrive.
