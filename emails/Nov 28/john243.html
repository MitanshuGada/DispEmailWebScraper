For those of you who are new to the sugar bowl, I know there's probably one thing that gets stuck in your craw, and may indeed affect whether you decide to engage, or simply write sugar dating off as something that other people do, and go back to your previous method of meeting women online.

You're not rich.  

Sure, you've probably been curious about sugar dating, after hearing whispers about it, or catching a banner ad, or because you have had friends tell you about it. The idea of it was tantalizing: Spending time with beautiful young women, on your terms, and on your schedule.  What's not to like?  

When your curiosity became too much to bear, you probably decided to see if sugar dating was everything that you hoped it would be. You created a profile, and started browsing Secret Benefits to see what was out there.  That's probably where you discovered, to your chagrin, the same sorts of verbiage, popping up, time and time again:

"A guy who takes me around the world"

"Regular source of assistance"

"Pamper me in high style" 

That's when it began to dawn on you that this type of thing must only be for the rich.

“I can't afford this kind of thing,” you think.  “At least, not with regularity.” You may be able to picture sugar dating as an occasional luxury, a treat for yourself perhaps, but that's it - All of these women seem to be expecting a lot more extravagance than are capable of.

Don’t write off sugar dating so quickly.  These girls are listing their hopes and dreams perhaps, but these are not absolutes.  Not only that, these women who "prefer the finer things in life" do not speak for all sugar babies. Some sugar babies have expectations that are far more your speed... All you need to do is ask.  Start conversations, and see where they go.  Once you're getting to know each other, you and your prospective sugar baby can collaborate on your arrangement, and only then do you decide if it’s going to work out.   

Try it. Introduce yourself to a few ladies who strike your fancy, and find out for yourself if you have what it takes to make it as a sugar daddy. You have absolutely nothing to lose.

Yours,

Herb Rose
