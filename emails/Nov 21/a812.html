To purchase your Motor Vehicle, the buyer has applied for financing with Angle Finance. Before approving and releasing the funds, the lender requires a Verimoto Inspection to be completed to verify the asset and its current owner. This process is much faster and easier than a physical inspection, and will help sell your Motor Vehicle sooner.

Please follow the steps below to complete the inspection.

  1. Open the Verimoto Web-App in your mobile phone internet browser - https://go.verimoto.com/g993Qv
  2. Enter your Inspection ID [4536] and email address or mobile phone number to receive a 6-digit one time password.
  3. Enter the one time password and follow the prompts.
  4. Capture and upload the required information. You’ll need to have the following handy:
    • The Vehicle being sold
    • Photo Identification
    • Vehicle Details
    • Rego Papers
    • Funds Destination

If your Motor Vehicle is fully or partially under finance, please upload an active payout letter from your finance company as part of this inspection. This along with your Funds Destination details will allow the buyer’s finance company to settle with your finance company and to pay any balance directly into your nominated bank account.

Please note, we take the security of your personal data very seriously. All information uploaded via the Verimoto Web-App is encrypted and will be purged from our database when no longer required for the purpose of verification. You can find more information about how we protect your data here Your Personal Data is SAFE with Verimoto.

Please refer to the Seller Help Guide for comprehensive assistance in completing the process successfully. In addition to the aforementioned guide, we recommend exploring the support resources available on the Verimoto Help page.

If needed, you can reach our support team on support@verimoto.com or via the in-app chat function. You can also contact Angle Finance Admin, who requested the Verimoto Inspection on ffanglefinanceadmin@yopmail.com
